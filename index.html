About Hima

The Golden Girl from Assam

Born on January 9, 2000, in the small village of Dhing in Assam, Hima Das represents the spirit of every woman who dares to dream beyond boundaries. Known as the "Dhing Express," she has blazed a trail of success in track events, becoming an icon for women's empowerment through sports.

From humble beginnings as a daughter of a rice farmer, Hima initially played football before her exceptional speed caught the attention of local coaches. Her journey exemplifies how determination and talent can break through gender barriers and societal limitations.

In 2018, she made history by becoming the first Indian athlete to win a gold medal in a track event at the IAAF World U20 Championships. This groundbreaking achievement not only catapulted her to national fame but also inspired countless young girls across India to pursue their dreams in sports.
